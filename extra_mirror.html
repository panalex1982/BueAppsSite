Guide of Extra Mirror!

The current functionality via photos.

Zoom In

Touch from down to up.
Touch and move your finger from down to up, in the camera preview area for Zoom In. Some cameras does not support the zoom function, if this happen the application will inform you.

Zoom Out

Touch from up to down.
Touch and move your finger from up to down, in the camera preview area for Zoom In..

Change Camera

Check your camera.
Check the appropriate camera and the application will close the other and open your camera. If there is no box checked then a white backround will take place.

Open the settings

Touch the three squares.
The button the picture shows, opens a bar with a settings and exit options. Touch it again and the menu will close.
